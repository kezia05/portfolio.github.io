About The Artist


Kezia Cauchi is a 20 year old Maltese student who is currently reading for her Bachelor of Fine Arts in Digital Arts, at the University of Malta.

From a very young age of 6, she started her artistic practice, where her skills further developed as she obtained Art at Ordinary Level, GCSE and at Advanced Level.

With her versatile and creative style, working in Fine Arts, Graphic Design, Illustration and Photography, she aspires to work as a full-time Graphic Designer, whilst experimenting more in the Fine Arts industry.
